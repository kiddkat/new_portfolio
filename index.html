About

I'm always making something—whether it is in my head trying to craft a strategy to solve a design problem. Or in the kitchen stirring up a finger licking feast. Whatever it is that I'm making, I always do my best to hit the sweet spot.

Research + Strategy

My curiosity of people and their behaviors drives my passion for problem solving and inspires me to ideate on ways to make life better.

Design + Storytelling

Storytelling informs my design process. It enables me to sketch personas, user flows and scenarios that personify meaningful human connections.

Testing + Iterating

I practice rapid prototyping to verify a design, communicate an idea and fix design issues early on, thus preventing costly changes later.

Design Perspective

Good design is design that changes behavior for the better. I think it needs to take into account the context of the environment, of the human condition, the culture, and then attempt to make the things you do—make us do them better, make us do better things. It encourages us to change the way that we live.—Jon Kolko

Portfolio

As a recent graduate of General Assembly's User Experience Design Immersive program, I bring a broad range of skills from user research, strategy, concept ideation, rapid prototyping to visual design.

I believe good user experience is rooted to a deep understanding of the motivation, challenges and goals of the user as well as the company who builds the product and services.

My portfolio is a journey into this design thinking and problem solving process. It is about integrating user experience principles with my visual design background to create elegant and intuitive experiences that empower people.
